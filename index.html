Who We Are

About
BharatMUN

BharatMUN stands as a beacon of opportunity — an entirely student-led initiative dedicated to bringing the transformative power of Model United Nations to every corner of our nation.

We believe in inclusivity: that every student, regardless of background or geography, deserves access to the tools that will shape them into the leaders of tomorrow. That's why we reach beyond urban centres into the rural heartlands, breaking down barriers and opening doors.

With three years of legacy, BharatMUN is renowned for high-impact conferences that uphold the highest standards of academic excellence, professionalism, and inclusivity. Each edition is meticulously curated with engaging agendas, diverse committees, and an environment that encourages critical thinking.

By blending rigorous debate with mentorship-driven learning, BharatMUN ensures participants not only understand global issues but develop the confidence and leadership skills to address them.

The Founder's Desk

Rishaan Sahgal

Founder & President

"Do what others won't today,
so you can do what others can't tomorrow."

BharatMUN was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that every young Indian, regardless of where they come from, deserves a seat at the table. As Founder and President, I've spent three years building a conference that doesn't just debate world issues — it creates world-class debaters, diplomats, and leaders.

From our inaugural chapter to what we're building with Chapter 4.0, every edition has been driven by one mission: to empower the next generation of Indian voices on the global stage. Our conferences are entirely student-led, proving that when young people are given responsibility, they exceed every expectation.

Chapter 4.0 is our most ambitious edition yet. We invite delegates from across the country to Gurugram this August for two days of unparalleled intellectual discourse, mentorship, and community. Come ready to speak, debate, lead — and leave transformed.

Our Reach

Impact

BharatMUN aims to take the transformative power of soft-skills to the farthest corners of our nation. We believe talent is universal but opportunity is not. Given the right platform, young people can develop the confidence, communication & leadership needed to create agency for themselves and their communities. In the 21st century, soft-skills are not just valuable, they're essential. By making them accessible to all, BharatMUN aims to unlock incredible untapped potential across Bharat.

Today, we stand as partners of more than ten NGOs working towards education and skill-development of the underprivileged. Our founder and core team members are empanelled as Public Speaking Coaches at several organisations, and actively take public speaking workshops and training sessions for the children. Our most prominent partnerships and collaborations include those with Umeed Foundation, Samidha Welfare Foundation and Udayan Care. We were also invited by Akashvani FM Rainbow 102.6 MHz in 2024 on the occasion of UN Day to be interviewed for our impact on uplifting Indian youth, and the interview was broadcasted to lakhs of listeners across the country.

With 2000+ net conference attendees, 5000+ trained students from partner institutions, 3000+ trained children from NGOs and an active audience of 20,000+ students from across the nation on our social media handles, BharatMUN has impacted countless young lives and strives to continue its mission to provide a platform to every student across the nation.
